User Guide

Download the Dataset

Go to the home page, click the download the dataset button in the center

Use the Visualization

Use Search Districts to search the name of school districts you want to visualize

View your search result under All Districts

View attributes the dataset contains and select years under All attributes

Click expand/minimize under Terminology to show/hide the window that shows the explaination of terminologies in the page

Select the attribute to visualize and view the visualized data under California School District Data Visualization

Put your mouse on the points to show year and enrollment data
